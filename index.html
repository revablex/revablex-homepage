How it works

Four simple steps to understand exactly what your audience is asking — powered by Google OAuth and AI analysis.

1

Connect Your YouTube Account

Sign in securely with Google OAuth. We request read-only access to your YouTube channel — no passwords stored.

2

Select a Video

Browse your uploaded videos and pick the one you want to analyze. We support any public or unlisted video on your channel.

3

Fetch & Analyze Comments

RevableX fetches all comments and runs AI analysis to detect patterns, themes, and questions from your audience.

4

Get Audience Insights

See a clean summary of what your viewers are asking most — sorted by frequency and intent, ready to act on.

You decide what RevableX can access. Revoke permissions at any time from your Google account settings — we respect your control completely.

Your comment data and insights are yours alone. We never sell, share, or expose your data to any third-party services.
