Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026  ·  Contact: teamrevablex@gmail.com

Introduction

RevableX is a tool that helps creators analyze YouTube comments and discover audience insights. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our platform.

Information We Collect

When you use RevableX, we collect the following types of information:

  • Google Account Information: Your name, email address, and profile picture, collected through Google OAuth for authentication purposes.
  • YouTube Comments: Comments from YouTube videos that you explicitly select for analysis.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform features, to improve your experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect exclusively to provide and improve our services:

  • Analyze comments from your selected YouTube videos to detect audience questions and topics
  • Identify patterns, trends, and recurring questions in your audience
  • Generate monetization insights and product recommendations based on audience demand
  • Improve our AI algorithms and platform functionality

YouTube API Services Usage

RevableX uses YouTube API Services to access comments from videos selected by the user. Our use of YouTube data is governed by the following principles:

  • Read-Only Access: We only read YouTube comments. We never modify, delete, or post any content on your behalf.
  • No Data Selling or Sharing: Your YouTube data is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties.
  • Analysis Only: YouTube data is used solely for generating audience insights within RevableX.
  • User-Initiated: We only access data from videos that you explicitly select for analysis.

RevableX's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. You can also review Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data transmission using HTTPS/TLS protocols
  • Secure storage of OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Limited access to personal data by authorized personnel only
  • Secure authentication through Google OAuth 2.0

Third-Party Services

RevableX integrates with the following third-party services to provide its functionality:

  • Google OAuth: For secure user authentication.
  • YouTube API: For accessing comments from your selected videos.
  • Google Gemini AI: For analyzing and clustering audience questions.

Each of these services has its own privacy policy and terms. We encourage you to review them.

User Control & Data Deletion

You have full control over your data at all times:

  • You can revoke RevableX's access to your YouTube account at any time through your Google Account permissions.
  • You can disconnect your YouTube account from within the RevableX platform.
  • You can request deletion of all your data by contacting us at teamrevablex@gmail.com.
  • Upon account deletion, all associated data including analyses and connected platform data will be permanently removed.

Other Platform Integrations

RevableX may expand to support additional platforms beyond YouTube in the future (such as Discord, Twitter/X, or other creator platforms). When new integrations are added, this Privacy Policy will be updated to reflect any additional data collection or usage. We will notify users of significant changes.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through the platform. Continued use of RevableX after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at teamrevablex@gmail.com.
