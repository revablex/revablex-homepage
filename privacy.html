01 Introduction

Welcome to RevableX. RevableX is a web-based tool that helps YouTube creators and marketers analyze comments on their videos and extract meaningful audience insights — such as frequently asked questions, common feedback, and trending topics.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use RevableX, how we use that information, and the steps we take to keep it safe. By using RevableX, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with care and transparency. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

02 Information We Collect

When you sign in to RevableX using your Google account, we may access the following information:

Google Account Information
  • Your name and profile picture (used to personalize your experience)
  • Your email address (used for account identification and communication)
YouTube Data
  • Comments from YouTube videos you explicitly select within the app
  • Basic video metadata (title, video ID) of selected videos
  • No access to your private data, watch history, subscriptions, or financial information

We do not collect any information beyond what is necessary to provide the core functionality of RevableX.

03 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to operate and improve RevableX. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Authenticate your identity and provide secure access to your account
  • Retrieve and analyze YouTube comments from videos you select
  • Identify audience questions, feedback patterns, and trending topics in your comments
  • Generate insights and summaries that help you understand your viewers
  • Improve the accuracy and performance of our analysis tools
  • Respond to support requests or inquiries you send us

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the service you requested.

04 YouTube Data Usage

RevableX uses YouTube API Services to access comments from videos explicitly selected by the user via Google OAuth. Our access is strictly read-only — we never modify, delete, or upload any content on your behalf.

Here is exactly what RevableX does — and does not do — with your YouTube data:

✓ What we do
  • Read comments from YouTube videos you explicitly choose to analyze
  • Process those comments within the app to surface insights and questions
  • Display analysis results to you within your RevableX session
✗ What we never do
  • Modify, edit, or delete any YouTube comments or content
  • Upload content or post on your behalf
  • Access videos or channels you have not explicitly selected
  • Sell, share, or distribute your YouTube data to any third party
  • Use your YouTube data for advertising or marketing purposes
  • Retain your YouTube data beyond what is needed for the session

By using RevableX, you also agree to be bound by the YouTube Terms of Service and acknowledge that our use of the YouTube API is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

05 Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your data seriously. Our approach to data storage and protection includes:

  • We collect and store only the minimum data necessary to provide our service
  • Data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections at all times
  • We do not retain YouTube comment data beyond your active session unless required for analysis you explicitly requested
  • Access to stored data is restricted to authorized team members only
  • We regularly review our security practices to ensure they remain up to date

While we take reasonable precautions to protect your information, no system is completely immune to security risks. We encourage you to use strong passwords and keep your Google account secure.

06 Third-Party Services

RevableX relies on the following third-party services to function:

  • Google OAuth 2.0 — Used for secure sign-in and authentication. When you sign in with Google, you authorize RevableX to access specific scopes of your account as described above.
  • YouTube Data API v3 — Used to retrieve comment data from YouTube videos you select. All API usage complies with YouTube's API Services Terms of Service.

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review the Google Privacy Policy to understand how Google handles your data.

We do not integrate with any advertising networks, data brokers, or analytics platforms that have access to your personal information.

07 Your Control and Rights

You are in control of your data. At any time, you can:

  • Revoke access — Remove RevableX's access to your Google account at any time by visiting your Google Account Permissions page.
  • Request deletion — Contact us at teamrevablex@gmail.com to request deletion of any personal data we hold.
  • Access your data — Request a summary of what personal data we have associated with your account.
  • Opt out — Stop using RevableX at any time, which ends our data collection immediately.

We will respond to all data-related requests within 30 days. Revoking access through Google will immediately prevent RevableX from accessing any new data from your account.

08 Other Platforms

RevableX currently focuses on YouTube comment analysis. However, we plan to expand support to additional platforms — such as other video hosting or social media services — in the future.

When such integrations are introduced, the same core privacy principles will apply:

  • Access will be limited to data you explicitly authorize
  • We will use read-only access wherever possible
  • No data will be sold or shared with third parties
  • You will retain full control to revoke access at any time

We will update this Privacy Policy to reflect any new integrations before they become available to users, and we will notify you of any significant changes.

09 Contact Information

10 Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of RevableX after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

If we make any material changes that affect how we handle your personal data, we will make reasonable efforts to notify you — for example, by sending an email to the address associated with your account.
